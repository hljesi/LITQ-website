How to Join

LITQ welcomes applications from prospective master's and PhD students, as well as prospective interns and postdoctoral fellows, interested in theoretical computer science or quantum information. Prospective master's students or interns are typically encouraged to join a computer science (or physics) master's program at Université de Montréal before contacting professors for the master's thesis. (The exception is if a student already has significant research experience in the relevant field – in this case proceed in the same way as a PhD applicant). PhD and postdoctoral applicants can find advertised positions here or can contact a faculty member briefly outlining your research interests, and including a CV and transcripts.

Prospective PhD students should have a background in mathematics, computer science, or physics. All positions come with funding attached.
