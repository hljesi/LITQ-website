How to Apply

Information about open positions

LITQ welcomes applications for prospective Masters and PhD students interested in theoretical computer science or quantum information. Prospective masters students typically are encouraged to join the computer science or physics program at Université de Montréal before contacting professors for the masters thesis. PhD applicants can find advertised positions here or contact a faculty member briefly outlining your research interests, and including your CV and transcripts.

Prospective PhD students should have a background in mathematics, computer science, or physics. All positions come with funding attached.
