About LITQ

Where theory meets
the quantum frontier

Welcome to the website of the Laboratory of Theoretical and Quantum Computing (LITQ) in the Department of Computer Science and Operations Research (DIRO) at the University of Montreal. Our laboratory has six faculty members and is very active in both research and teaching.

We work on various aspects related to the foundations of computer science and physics. Our research focuses on classical and quantum cryptography, computational complexity, quantum computing, and graph theory.

The LITQ is always looking for dynamic students at all levels and loves to welcome visitors.

Join LITQ

Information about
open positions

LITQ welcomes applications for prospective Masters and PhD students interested in theoretical computer science or quantum information. Prospective masters students typically are encoraged to join the computer science or physics program at Université de Montréal before contacting professors for the masters thesis. PhD applicants can find advertised positions here or contact a faculty member briefly outlining your research interests, and including your CV and transcripts.

Prospective PhD students should have a background in mathematics, computer science, or physics. All positions come with finding attatched.
